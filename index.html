Mr.Beast

Mr. Beast is a very popular channel on YouTube who’s creator uses the millions of dollars of revenue produced from the channel to make really fun and interesting videos about donating to things like charities, animal shelters, etc. and doing other genuinely amazing things for society. For example, he once bought every item from a local grocery store with the money he earned from his channel and donated everything to a homeless shelter. He also hosts really fun events that collaborate with a wide range of popular YouTubers so his content touches on a variety of categories. Mr. Beasts content is family friendly, entertaining, and inspiring, and every subscription he gets allows him to do bigger and crazier charitable videos. "Netflix Expands Into a World Full of Censors" - New York Times

Through my research relating to the podcast episode I worked on with Communication Conundrum, I found issues of Netflix censorship in Turkey to be particularly interesting. The Turkish government holds Netflix accountable for these issues and Netflix has a lot of problems streaming popular shows in the country for a number of reasons. The New York Times posted an article about a range of instances in which certain shows in Netflix have been censored because of things like sexuality, indecency, etc. This article describes the troubles Netflix has with navigating these obstacles and their responses to issues of censorship as well. Petfinder

Recently I have adopted two kittens, and the process was a lot more challenging than I thought it would be. I took months finding my kittens and researching what I needed to give them the best life they could have. One resource I used over and over again was Petfinder. I found my kittens on this website and I used a lot of their advice like their pre-adoption checklist to learn about the process. On this website, you can search for a range of different pets in any specific area and you can filter them by age, color, etc. A lot of animal shelters and breeders use this website so you will definitely find the perfect companion for you here.
